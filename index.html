Vision

We build leaders who actually do stuff and not just talk. MECA FBLA teaches real skills, such as projects, pitches, and teamwork, which gets us into competitions and scholarships, and hands leadership to the next person stronger than we found it. Join if you want to learn, lead, and leave a legacy.
